📄 Paper Presentation – Rules & Conditions

Participation

  • Participants can join individually or in a team (max 4 members)
  • Only one paper per team is allowed

Submission Rules

  • Paper must be original and not copied
  • Abstract submission is mandatory before full paper
  • File format: PDF / Word
  • Follow proper format (title, abstract, content, conclusion, references)

Presentation Rules

  • Each team gets 5–7 minutes for presentation
  • 2–3 minutes for Q&A session
  • Exceeding time limit may lead to negative marking

Presentation Requirements

  • PPT is mandatory
  • Bring your presentation in pen drive + backup (email/phone)
  • Use clear slides with minimal text and visuals

Judging Criteria

  • Innovation & originality
  • Technical/content knowledge
  • Presentation skills
  • Clarity and confidence
  • Response to questions

General Conditions

  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited
  • Any form of misconduct leads to disqualification
  • Participants must report 30 minutes before the event
  • Bring college ID card

Results & Certificates

  • Judges' decision will be final and binding
  • Certificates will be provided for all participants
  • Prizes for winners and runners
